ABOUT OUR DETERMINATION

Feliks Kasmi

Founder

Feliks Kasmi founded Tusity with the firm idea that the hotel industry was obsolete and its value proposition towards travellers needed a radical change, driven by the new era of digitization. Tusity has grown rapidly, driven by the demand of travellers looking for a new concept of hospitality, focused on people, in proximity to the local community and in greater social interaction between customers, travellers and locals.

WORK WITH US

Working at Tusity means working for people with passion and motivation, as a team, in solidarity and thinking about the most important thing: the good life.

Our history began with 3 coliving and coworking in the Canary Islands, but our business model is expanding. Therefore, working at Tusity is a job challenge, an opportunity to grow personally and professionally.

Why is working with us different? Because we care about our people and create a work environment with everything but routine.
